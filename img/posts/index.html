Services

With our services we want to facilitate the adoption of the NDC standard for the air travel distribution industry

NDC Gateway

Our NDC Gateway aggregator enables OTAs, TMCs or Fare Consolidators to adopt the NDC standard seamless, faster, at a lower costs.

  • NDC-compliant real-time aggregation
  • Anonymous/Personalised Shopping
  • NDC implementations isolation (SOAP, REST)
  • Authentication methods isolation
  • Ancilliaries Normalization between airlines

NDC Analytics

Our NDC Analytics suite provides a better view and understanding on your NDC Channel. It's flexible and customizable to track any data on your NDC channel.

  • NDC-focused analytics
  • Technical and Business relevant KPIs
  • Customizable KPI's
  • Customizable display and dashboards

NDC Monitor

A service to monitor 24/7 your NDC channel observing performance and raising alerts when indicators go out of range.

  • NDC-focused indicators
  • Technical and Business relevant indicators
  • Customizable indicators and ranges
  • API for 3rd party systems integration
